Orem Chamber of Commerce Site Plan

Site Name

Orem Chamber of Commerce - A vibrant community's heart, fostering business growth and networking.

Site Purpose

To serve as a central hub for local businesses to network, grow, and thrive through advocacy, resources, and community development.

  • Networking opportunities and events.
  • Business resources and advocacy.
  • Community and economic development initiatives.

Scenarios

  • How can my business benefit from Chamber membership?
  • What networking events are available this month?
  • How can I contact the Chamber for partnership opportunities?

Color Schema

Primary Color: #005AAB - Used for headings, buttons, and links.

Secondary Color: #F2F2F2 - Used for background accents and section dividers.

Typography

Primary Font: Roboto - Applied across the site for consistency and readability.
